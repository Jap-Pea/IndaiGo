Mobile Banking — Quick Actions

Problem. Busy users need 1–3 minute tasks (balance, favorite transfer, saved bill) with fewer taps and better one-hand reach.

Goals. Balance→Transfer to favorite ≤3 taps; Quick Actions discoverability +40%; tap targets ≥48dp; contrast ≥4.5:1.

Primary tasks

  • Check balance (home peek)
  • Transfer to favorite (long-press account → Quick Transfer)
  • Pay saved bill (Quick Actions row)
  • Freeze card (hold to confirm)

Key decisions

  • Thumb-first layout: actions in bottom third
  • Friction for destructive: hold-to-freeze + confirm
  • Progressive disclosure for advanced options

Accessibility & states

  • 48×48dp tap targets, visible focus rings
  • Haptics: soft tap; warning on destructive
  • Offline shows cached balances + “Updated Xm ago”

Success metrics

  • Taps to favorite transfer ≤3
  • Quick Actions CTR ≥55% (returning)
  • NPS “ease of use” +15

Analytics Dashboard — Case Study

Problem: Dense tables, low contrast, and no keyboard nav.

Approach: Type scale + spacing system, focus rings, cardized modules.

  • 12/16/20 type ramp, min 4.5:1 contrast
  • ARIA landmarks & skip-to-content
  • Breakpoints at 480 / 768 / 1024
