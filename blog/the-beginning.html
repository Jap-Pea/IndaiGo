The Beginning

Where it all began...

As a kid, I was obsessed with Pokémon® on the Game Boy®. Hours would disappear while I trained my team and explored those pixelated worlds.

At the same time, I had a first-generation PlayStation®, and I couldn't stop wondering - why wasn't there a 3D version of Pokémon? The idea of running around in a fully 3D world with creatures battling and interacting felt like magic waiting to happen.

That thought stuck with me for years - the "what if" of combining those worlds.

Now, I've reached the point where I can actually build that spark into reality. What started as childhood curiosity is becoming the foundation of my journey into game development.

Why I'm doing this

I'm aiming to learn it all from — HTML, JavaScript, C++, and building immersive experiences with Unreal Engine®.

"IndaiGo"—what's the deal?

IndaiGo is my personal brand and umbrella for my projects. It's small now, but it's growing.
