The Beginning

Where it all began.

As a kid, I was obsessed with Pokémon® on the Game Boy®.

I also had the first-generation PlayStation®, and I always wondered why there wasn't a 3D version of Pokémon. That thought stuck with me for years.

Now, it's time to revisit that spark and actually build.

Why I'm doing this

I’m aiming to learn it all — HTML, JavaScript, C++, and building immersive experiences with Unreal Engine®.

‘IndaiGo’—what’s the deal?

IndaiGo is my personal brand and umbrella for my projects. It’s small now, but it’s growing.
